# Pretotyping and Prototyping 

Learn how to build pretotypes and prototypes as tools for testing and validating the desirability, viability and feasibility of your solution. 

At the end of this module, you will have validated the desirability, viability & feasibility of the solution you developed in Ideating Solutions. The module uses real-world examples to inspire your hypotheses and tests.

## Common Mistakes

- Skipping validation and investing too much in the wrong idea
- Relying on oral feedback instead of behaviour
- Over-engineering prototypes

**Duration:** 2-4 weeks
