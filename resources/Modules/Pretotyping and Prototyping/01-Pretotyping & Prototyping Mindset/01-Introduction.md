# Introduction

In Pretotyping & Prototyping, we shift gears and emphasise solution research over customer research. The solution you ideated in the module 'Ideating Solutions' is the starting point for your solution research. As part of Pretotyping and Prototyping, we will strive to build confidence in three areas:

1. **Desirability** – Is my solution desirable?
2. **Viability** – Can I build a viable business model for this solution?
3. **Feasibility** – Can I build the solution? And if so, can I build it in such a way that my users understand it, and it fits into my users’ routines? (The last part is sometimes referred to as usability).

![In Pretotyping & Prototyping, we will answer whether the ideated solution is desirable, can yield a viable business and can be built.](/api/media/file/M4-L1T1-InnovationFramework.png)

The goal is to validate the solution you developed in *Ideating Solutions* based on what you have uncovered in *A Problem Worth Solving*. Once you have a validated solution, you are ready to attract funding.
