# Finalising and Closing Your Round

Once you've secured a lead VC, the next step is to finalise and close your funding round.

### **Gap Filling and Closing**

After a lead VC commits, they typically fund the majority of the round (80-90%). The remaining gap can be filled with additional angel investors. This requires careful consideration, as you should select investors who align with your vision and provide value beyond just capital. Once your choices are made, you can invite remaining investors to participate as followers, which helps finalize the round with the right mix of supporters. The entire closing process, including legal formalities, can take several months.

### **The Fundraising Timeline**

The complete fundraising process, from your initial outreach to having funds in your bank account, can take three months or longer. It's therefore critical to align your runway with this timeline. We recommend starting the fundraising process six to nine months before your runway ends. This proactive approach ensures you have a sufficient buffer to handle potential delays and avoid the risk of running out of capital.
