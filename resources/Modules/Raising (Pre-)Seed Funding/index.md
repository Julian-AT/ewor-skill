# Raising (Pre-)Seed Funding

Determine your fundraising strategy, develop the required materials, and discover outreach best practices.

At the end of this module, you will (be ready to) raise your (Pre-)Seed Round. You will have identified the right investors, created an investor deck, and set up a clear outreach strategy with a supporting CRM.

## Common Mistakes

- Running out of cash
- Underestimating the time required to fundraise
- Burning leads and intros due to insufficient prep

**Duration:** 4-12 weeks
