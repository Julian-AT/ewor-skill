# How Much to Raise?

To determine how much capital to raise, we recommend the following steps.

## Step 1: Define Your Target Milestone

Define the key milestone you aim to achieve with the raised funds. The milestone should be significant enough to, when achieved, significantly increase the valuation at which you can raise your next round while also being attainable within an 18-month timeframe. This milestone will be the cornerstone of your pitch to investors. It communicates to investors how their investment will directly contribute to your startup's progress and increased valuation for future funding rounds. Common milestones include:

- Launch: Achieving a market-ready product or service.
- Traction: Demonstrating exponential user growth and engagement.
- Product-Market Fit: Validating that your product meets customer needs, evidenced by paying customers who are retained and scalable growth.
- Key Performance Indicators (KPIs): Showing strong metrics like CAC/LTV ratios and profitability, even before full product-market fit.

## Step 2: Model Associated Costs

Develop a detailed financial model that outlines the expenses required to reach your chosen milestone. This model should include all necessary costs, ensuring a realistic assessment of your funding needs. Make sure to model additional staff requirements, hosting costs, cloud credits and costs that could scale significantly.

## Step 3: Determine a Funding Range

When determining your funding range, plan for at least 18 months of operational expenses. This provides sufficient time to reach your milestone without the constant pressure of immediate fundraising. A shorter runway increases fundraising frequency, thereby distracting you from building your product, while a longer runway can lead to excessive dilution.

To determine your funding range, determine the minimum and maximum funding amounts needed.

- **Minimum Funding:** Covers essential expenses to achieve the immediate milestone. This includes core operational costs like salaries, key hires, and necessary technology.
- **Maximum Funding:** Encompasses a broader range of expenses, enabling faster growth and multiple milestone achievements. This may include marketing, expansion, and additional operational costs.

When determining your target amount, consider both the benchmarks for your stage, geography, and industry as well as the investor climate. More about that in the next topic.
