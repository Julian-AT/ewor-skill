# The Foundation of Personal Branding

[YouTube](https://youtu.be/XtunnDKqn8Y)

Your personal brand is what people say about you when you are not in the room. The goal of this course is to influence your personal brand at scale by sharing relevant and engaging content. In this context, we recommend you showcase your expertise, values, personality and personal story. 

- **Content Engine**
The content engine is a process through which you turn pillar content into micro-content.** Pillar content **is a long-form piece of content such as a blog, podcast, interview or keynote presentation.
- **Micro-content** are individual pieces of content taken from pillar content. For example, one podcast episode can inspire multiple LinkedIn posts and TikTok videos. 
- **Your LinkedIn Profile**
Your LinkedIn profile is often the first thing people see when they google you. Before starting to interact with your audience, make sure your LinkedIn profile is attractive and up-to-date. In your ‘about’ section, we recommend you add a one-liner about the problem you are solving, a few lines on how you got where you are and what it is you are doing today. Lastly, include a call to action. 
- **Your Content Archetype**
Your content archetype highlights the key topics that you will talk about in your content. There are 4 pillars: tactics, aspirational stories, insights and personal stories. When building your content archetype, consider what content would appeal to your target audience.
