# Personal Branding

Discover how to build a personal brand that drives trust and growth—through content, community, and smart use of data.

Welcome to Personal Branding! In this course, Marvin Sanginés will talk you through all the steps to develop and scale your personal brand with the goal of expanding your network and developing trust with relevant stakeholders. The course touches upon developing a content strategy, a recommended content production process, as well as community management and analytics.
