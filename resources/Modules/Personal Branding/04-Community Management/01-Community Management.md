# Community Management

[YouTube](https://youtu.be/8RhAFsQs5uY)

To make the most out of your content, Marvin recommends trying to turn attention into conversations and ultimately conversions. 

There are three strategies to consistently start new conversations:

1. **Network expansions**

Send out connection requests or follow people who fit your target audience. 

1. **Target engagement**

Choose specific accounts such as thought leaders in your field with which you consistently engage.

1. **Account routine**

Reach out to and message people who are interacting with your profile or content. In addition, make sure to always respond to DMs.

## Action Steps

1. Go back to your notion template and update your variables and metrics
2. Update your list of thought leaders and creators you want to engage with
3. Schedule your network expansion session
4. Turn on post notifications for the thought leaders and creators you want to engage with and comment on a few of their posts
5. Schedule your account routine
