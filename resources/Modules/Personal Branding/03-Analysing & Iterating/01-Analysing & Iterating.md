# Analysing & Iterating

[YouTube](https://youtu.be/w5TwEUI4M4k)

Analyse your post performance to determine what content resonates with your target audience, then iterate on your strategy if necessary. Some quantitative metrics that you can track are:

- New followers
- Profile views
- Post impressions
- Click-through rates
- Demographics of your audience

Additionally, aim to understand how people initially learn about you during sales calls or when you meet them at conferences. This is key for personal branding, as it offers insights into the effectiveness of your outreach channels and helps tailor your messaging and networking strategies.

When it comes to repurposing your content, analysing your posts is also helpful. Content that performed well, can be repurposed in a different format or even just reposted with minimal edits to ensure high engagement.

## Action Steps

1. Add all your published posts to your [notion page](https://notus-digital-products.notion.site/Content-Production-OS-03478485711c494ca9cbd281e5c755d1)
2. Block 90 minutes each month to analyse the quantitative metrics and iterate on your content cycle
3. Repurpose existing content
