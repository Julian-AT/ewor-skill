# Content Production

[YouTube]( https://youtu.be/mugcz_8MyZU)

In order to prepare for creating a content piece, we recommend the following steps:

1. Identify the core idea
2. Visualise your target audience
3. Determine which emotion you want to evoke

Next, create the first draft of your headline and your copy. Continue to edit and revise both until you are satisfied with the quality. Keep in mind that your headline is what entices people to keep reading, so Marvin recommends spending the majority of your time on your headline.

If possible, add visuals to your post, as it will be more likely to perform well. Aside from images, you can also use videos or make carousels.

[**AuthoredUp**](https://authoredup.com/)

Available as an app or as a Chrome extension. The tool helps you see how your post will look in the feed and stylise your post. In addition, it helps with post-analytics.

**ChatGPT Prompt**

ChatGPT can be great for editing and shortening your copy. You can use the following prompt to get some good results: “Act as my copy editor. Please optimise the following LinkedIn Post draft to be readable, clear and concise without changing the messaging.”

[**Beehiiv**](https://www.beehiiv.com/)

Marvin recommends the tool Beehiiv for newsletters rather than LinkedIn for newsletters.
