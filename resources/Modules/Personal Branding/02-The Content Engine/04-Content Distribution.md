# Content Distribution

[YouTube]( https://youtu.be/Lfi6GSLBlo4)

The key to building a strong personal brand is consistency. Therefore, it is important to set a posting cadence you can commit to.

Make sure to post your content at the time that your target audience is most likely to be on the platform. This is often early in the morning or around lunchtime. To improve the performance of your content piece, spend about 15 minutes before publishing it on the platform engaging with other people’s content and answering DMs. The algorithm will then favour your post for a higher reach.
