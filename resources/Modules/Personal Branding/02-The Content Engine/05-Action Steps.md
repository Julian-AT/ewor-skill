# Action Steps



[YouTube]( https://youtu.be/65tIE_72-EE)



Before continuing to the next lesson, Marvin recommends completing the following action steps:

1. Familiarise yourself with the ideation structure
2. Add all existing content sources to your [notion template](https://notus-digital-products.notion.site/Content-Production-OS-03478485711c494ca9cbd281e5c755d1)
3. Block recurring slots in your calendar for content calls or reflection
4. Block recurring ideation and content production slots to your calendar
5. Define your content team
6. Commit to a posting cadence
7. Hold your first content call
8. Host your first content production session
9. Write 4 content pieces and schedule them
