# Content Ideation

[YouTube](https://youtu.be/_IdgEp04_os)

The first step of the content engine is content ideation. Your goal is to create a backlog of great content and ideas that you can post later on.

There is likely to be a lot of inspiration in your content archetype. However, that is not the only source. You can also conduct a content call with a colleague or friend to discuss what happened in the past few weeks at the company or in the world to come up with new content ideas, conduct customer interviews to drive content clarity, or even create your own photo gallery.

Using this website, you can see what people are searching for most often on the web in a multitude of topics, as well as search popularity, search volume, and predicted cost-per-click. This can be another source of inspiration for your content ideation.
