# Final Thoughts on Personal Branding

[YouTube](https://youtu.be/O1OmsOVzzyU)

In this course, we discussed how to consistently build your personal brand through content production and distribution. By sticking to this process, your personal brand might become your start-up’s most valuable asset.

The process we discussed consists of the following:

- Adopting the content mindset
- Ideating content ideas, either by yourself or with your team
- Analysing content performance and iterating if needed
- Turning attention into conversations and conversions
