# Entrepreneurial Finance

Reach model-market fit by choosing the right business model and creating a financial model for your venture.

This module introduces you to the fundamentals of business modelling—why business models matter, the most common types, and how to apply them. You'll also gain an overview of key accounting principles and an introduction to various valuation methods. By the end, you’ll be able to build a financial model for your venture and estimate its value using different approaches. These skills will help you understand how much funding you need, what your venture is worth now, and what it could be worth in the future.

## Common Mistakes

- Crafting a business model that doesn't tell a compelling story 
- Building a financial model that’s too optimistic or too complex
- Failing to keep your financial model and key assumptions up to date
