Before you can become truly productive, you need clarity—on where you are, where you want to go, and how to get there. In this module, we use a simple yet powerful framework to boost your productivity and focus. It consists of three key pillars:

![Your starting point and vision are clear—it's the mid-term where goals shift and chaos lives.](/api/media/file/PD-L2T2-DefineYourStartPoint-1.png)

## Definition and Goal Setting

Where are you right now in terms of your skills, mindset, and resources? Where do you want to go—both in the long-term (vision) and short-term (goals)? Your long-term vision might be bold—changing an industry, impacting millions, or achieving financial independence. Your short-term goal might be something concrete like finding product-market fit. Once these are defined, you can begin to draw the line between them.

### Time Management: Are You Spending Time Effectively?

With your path defined, the next challenge is prioritising your time. This means:

- Aligning your daily tasks with your long-term direction
- Distinguishing between being efficient (doing things right) and effective (doing the right things)
- Eliminating distractions and focusing on what really moves the needle

### Energy Management: How Do You Keep Going?

Even with a perfect plan, you won’t execute it unless you have the energy. 

- Building routines that sustain motivation
- Creating systems that keep you moving—even when you don’t feel like it
- Managing mental and physical energy so you can consistently show up

## Support: Stay Accountable, Stay Aligned

Personal development is easier—and more powerful—when you’re not doing it alone. To stay consistent with your goals, time, and energy management:

- **Meet with your accountability partner**: In this module, you’ll be paired with another Fellow committed to personal growth—connect, share contact details, and get to know each other.
- **Set up a weekly check-in**: Aim for 30 minutes each week to reflect, share progress, and support one another.
- **Follow our suggested prompts**: We’ll provide weekly conversation starters, but feel free to tailor them to your goals and challenges.

A strong support system will help you stay focused, spot blind spots, and keep moving—even when motivation dips.
