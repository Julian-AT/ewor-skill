# Introduction to Personal Development

[YouTube](https://youtu.be/akc9Aw-L9Jc)

Small habits, decisions, and mindset shifts can trigger powerful positive feedback loops. These loops lead to more opportunities, better outcomes, and a growing edge over time.

While success involves both luck and skill, your personal development is one of the few things fully within your control. And when done right, it becomes your biggest long-term advantage as a founder.

This module exists to help you take ownership of that with the EWOR Productivity Framework. We recommend working through this module with an accountability partner — think another EWOR Fellow, your co-founder, or a friend. If you are completing this module in parallel with us, we have already matched you with an accountability partner.
