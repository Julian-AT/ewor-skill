# Personal Development

Develop the mindset and routines to grow as a founder while staying focused, energised, and on track.

By the end of this module, you will have set meaningful personal and venture-related goals and built sustainable habits for managing your time, as well as your physical and mental energy in pursuit of those goals.

## Common Mistakes

- Chasing productivity without clear goals
- Getting outcompeted for a lack of speed
- Burning out due to poor energy management
