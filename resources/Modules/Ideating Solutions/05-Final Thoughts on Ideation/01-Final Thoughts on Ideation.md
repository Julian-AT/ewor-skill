# Final Thoughts on Ideation

This module focused on selecting an idea that would solve the problem you have identified. We did this by:

- **Ideation:** Ideating lots of ideas through brainstorming and/or brainwriting. 
- **Developing Concepts:** Combining the ideas generated during ideation into concepts.
- **Idea Selection:** Applying several decision-making frameworks to the list of generated ideas to determine which one is most promising and most interesting for you.

The goal of this module was to help you develop a truly innovative idea to solve the problem you identified and to ensure you can see yourself working on the solution for multiple years. 

Before moving on to prototyping, reflect on the questions and statements in the next topic. Discuss the outcome of your research and reflections with your coach and together decide whether you are ready for the next module.
