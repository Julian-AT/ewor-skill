# Discuss With Your Coach

Before moving on to the next module, schedule a meeting with your coach to discuss your progress and ensure you're ready to proceed. Come prepared to discuss the following:

## Your Process

- Walk your coach through the ideation sessions you have conducted and how many unique ideas you collected.
- Discuss the concepts you have developed.
- Share your process of narrowing down your ideas to the idea you have branded the winner. Explain which decision criteria you used and why.
- Present the idea you picked.

### Your Reflections

- Share your reflections on why you picked a certain idea as the winner. What excited you most about that idea? What are your worries?
- Be open and honest about any areas where you feel uncertain or need further guidance.
- Come prepared with specific questions for your coach to help clarify any doubts or concerns.

If you and your coach agree that you've developed a compelling idea or concept, you're ready to move on to the next module: Pretotyping and Prototyping.
