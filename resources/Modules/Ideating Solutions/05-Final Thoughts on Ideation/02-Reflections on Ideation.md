# Reflections on Ideation

Before moving on to the next module, we highly recommend that you reflect on the questions and statements below and discuss them with your coach.

### Have I done enough Ideation?

-  I have developed at least 10 HMW questions
-  I have hosted at least 3 ideation sessions with a variety of participants
-  Together, we have generated at least 100 unique ideas

### Are the developed concepts usable?

-  I have developed at least 3 different concepts
-  The concepts are concrete and complete

### Have I picked the right solution?

-  The solution solves the problem I have identified
-  The solution is technically feasible
-  I can think of several business models for the solution that would result in a viable business
-  My co-founders and I collectively possess the required skills to develop and market the solution
-  The solution differs meaningfully from available solutions by competitors
-  I can see myself working on this solution and the resulting business for at least 10 years
-  The solution excites me

If you have answered ‘no’ to any of the above questions or statements, we highly recommend spending some more time ideating, developing concepts or picking a solution.
