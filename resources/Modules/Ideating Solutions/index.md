# Ideating Solutions

Ideate potential solutions, develop concepts and select the most promising and exciting solution for prototyping.

At the end of this module, you will have selected a promising and exciting concept for the problem you have identified. This concept will be the starting point of Pretotyping and Prototyping.

## Common Mistakes

- Lack of passion for the solution
- Insignificant differentiation from existing solutions
- Waste of resources on unpromising solutions

**Duration:** 2-4 weeks
