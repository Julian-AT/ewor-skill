# Introduction

We're shifting from generating ideas (diverging) to selecting the best ones (converging). While it was crucial during the ideation phase to withhold judgment and generate as many innovative ideas as possible, in the idea selection phase, we will judge the ideas to identify a few promising ones.

**Please note **that with ‘ideas’ we do not only refer to the individual ideas that were generated during the ideation sessions but also to the concepts you designed in the previous lesson.
