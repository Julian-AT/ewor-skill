# Introduction to Researching Unavoidability

[YouTube](https://youtu.be/zTCqm3xvg3A)

For a problem to be worth solving (according to EWOR’s definition), the problem not only needs to be a true problem of high magnitude but also unavoidable. In previous topics, we discussed how need-finding interviews, observations and immersions lead to insights about workarounds, hacks and other solutions people use to solve the problem. In the following topics, we will discuss how researching potential solutions can give you an even clearer picture of the inescapability of the problem.
