# Discuss With Your Coach

Before moving on to the next module, schedule a meeting with your coach to discuss your progress and ensure you're ready to proceed. Come prepared to discuss the following:

**1. Your Research:**

- Walk your coach through the need-finding interviews, observations, and immersion experiences you conducted.
- Explain who you spoke with and how you found those people.

**2. Your Synthesis:**

- Bring any pictures, frameworks, diagrams, or other visuals you used to organize and analyse your research data.
- Be ready to discuss your thought process and get feedback from your coach. They might offer new perspectives or suggest alternative ways to synthesize your findings.

**3. Your Point of View:**

- Present your refined point of view (POV) – the core problem you identified.
- Explain how your research led you to this specific POV and why you believe it's a problem worth solving.
- Share what excites you about this problem and why you're motivated to solve it.

**4. Your Reflections:**

- Be open and honest about any areas where you feel uncertain or need further guidance.
- Come prepared with specific questions for your coach to help clarify any doubts or concerns.

If you and your coach agree that you've identified a compelling problem and demonstrated a strong founder-problem fit, you're ready to move on to the next module: Ideating Solutions.
