# Review and Remember

This module focused on validating whether the problem you had in mind is a problem worth solving. We did this by:

- **Need-finding Research:** Understanding the needs and frustrations of people experiencing the problem through interviews, observations and immersion.
- **Sizing the market:** Checking if enough people have this problem.
- **Looking at the competition:** Seeing how others are addressing this problem.

The goal of this research was to avoid the biggest startup risk: building something nobody wants! These methods are quick and affordable, perfect for this early stage. By synthesising your findings, you've formed a clear perspective on the problem. This will guide your brainstorming in the next module, where you'll start ideating solutions. Before moving on, reflect on the question and statements in the next topic. Discuss the outcome of your research and reflections with your coach and together decide whether you are ready for the next module.
