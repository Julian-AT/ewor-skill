In previous topics, we discussed need-finding as a research method to determine whether a problem is worth solving. Through interviews, observations and immersions you have determined whether individuals feel the problem deeply in terms of time, money or emotional distress. In the following topics, we will discuss how to determine whether the total amount of money people are willing to spend to solve the problem (market size or market opportunity), is high enough to warrant a possible VC investment. Many Venture Capital Firms won’t consider investing unless the market size or opportunity is or will soon reach over a billion euros per year.
