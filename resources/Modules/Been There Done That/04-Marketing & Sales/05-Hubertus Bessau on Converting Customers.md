## About Hubertus

[Hubertus Bessau](https://www.linkedin.com/in/hubertus/) is a serial entrepreneur dedicated to building purpose-driven companies that promote positive impact at scale. As the Co-Founder & Managing Director of PROJECT EADEN, he leads a mission to make saving the planet a pleasure, not a compromise. With a background in founding and managing successful ventures like mymuesli, where he served as Co-Founder & Managing Director for over 15 years, Hubertus brings extensive experience in building sustainable businesses. He is also a member of the Advisory Board at SIRPLUS and the Supervisory Board at mymuesli. Hubertus is committed to creating a better world through innovative and impactful initiatives.
