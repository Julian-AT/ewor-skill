## About Philipp

[Dr. Philipp Seybold](https://www.linkedin.com/in/philippsey/) is a business psychology scholar (Ph.D.), founder, and active angel investor. In 2014, he launched Combyne, a social network for fashion creations that grew to millions of users across the US, Europe, and Latin America, and successfully exited in 2022. Passionate about B2C, product strategy, scaling communities, and green tech, Philipp now applies his expertise as the founder and CEO of a new stealth startup.
