## About Omar

[Omar Hedeya](https://www.linkedin.com/in/omar-hedeya/) is an integral part of the Investment Team at 10x Founders, a network-driven investor supported by a community of 200+ entrepreneurs and angels. In his role, he focuses on identifying and investing in the most ambitious tech founders during Pre-seed, Seed, and Series A rounds, with the goal of nurturing them to build global tech leaders out of Europe.

Additionally, Omar actively participates as a Mentor at TUM.ai, a student initiative that bridges the gap between theoretical AI knowledge and practical application. He collaborates with industry leaders, works on internal projects, and contributes to AI education and the support of aspiring student entrepreneurs in the AI field.
