## About Paul

[Paul Müller](https://www.linkedin.com/in/paul1/), EWOR Partner and key advisor at Adjust, started his first company at 23 and got into the app business during its early days. Having success, he used tools that he built himself to start his second company, Adjust. Adjust was founded in 2012 in Berlin and over the next 10 years grew from 3 to 700+ people, raising over $200M, and sold for over $1B in 2021. Now, Paul helps teams avoid the mistakes he made throughout his entrepreneurial journey.

## Resources

Paul recommends the following resources to help you with systems architecture:

- [**Checkly**](https://www.checklyhq.com/) — A tool for monitoring your APIs and apps.
- [**Puppeteer**](https://github.com/puppeteer/puppeteer)** — **A GitHub repository that provides an API to replay and stringify recordings creating using Chrome DevTool Recorder.
- [**Zabbix**](http://zabbix.com/)** — **An open-source software tool to monitor IT infrastructure such as networks, servers, virtual machines and cloud services.
- [**Cloudwatch**](https://aws.amazon.com/cloudwatch/customers/)** — **As a good monitoring resource if you are mainly using AWS.
- [**Linux Professional Institute**](https://www.lpi.org/our-certifications/lpic-1-overview/)** — **Get your Linux certificates here.
