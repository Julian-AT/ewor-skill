## About Samuel

Next to studying Product Management as one of the first students of CODE University, he has bootstrapped a Product Studio to 15 full-time employees. With his company, he has helped more than 30 Start-Ups and established companies to launch digital products and some of them to put together high-performing product teams. Currently, he is transitioning into launching a product-based venture while driving the build-up of a high-performing product function at Vienna-based Series A med-tech start-up Platomics.

## Recommendations

In the video, Samuel recommends some books and software. You can find those below.

- **The Lean Startup by Eric Ries**
The famous book by Eric Ries lays out the Lean Startup method. The idea is to learn what your customers really want by continuously testing and adjusting your business. Focused more on learning through prototyping than conversations, the idea of rapid learning and innovation accounting can help you understand early customer discovery.
- **The Mom Test by Robert Fitzpatrick**
This book was written as a practical guide for the advice ‘talk to customers’. Robert Fitzpatrick uses lots of examples to help you understand how to conduct need-finding interviews.
