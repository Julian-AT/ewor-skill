# Been There Done That

Gain insights from successful entrepreneurs and investors as they share the hard-earned lessons from their journeys.

In this module, you’ll gain access to videos of successful entrepreneurs and investors sharing the hard-won lessons that shaped their journeys. Each story provides practical takeaways you can apply directly to your own path. The module will continue to grow over time, with new videos and insights added regularly.
