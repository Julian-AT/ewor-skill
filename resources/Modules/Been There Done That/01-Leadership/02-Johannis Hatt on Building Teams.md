## About Johannis

[Johannis Hatt](https://www.linkedin.com/in/johannishatt/), an accomplished serial entrepreneur and angel investor, founded and exited multiple marketing technology and SaaS companies. Notably, he co-founded a global SaaS firm, scaling it to over 300 employees and $25 million in ARR. Furthermore, two of the marketing technology ventures he spearheaded were acquired by leading publishing and marketplace entities.

With over a decade of investing experience, he has made 50+ angel investments in early-stage firms like CoachHub and Everphone. Additionally, he supports emerging managers and funds. Beyond business, Johannis is an avid art collector, focusing on street art, photography, and contemporary pieces – including works by Banksy and Shepard Fairey.
