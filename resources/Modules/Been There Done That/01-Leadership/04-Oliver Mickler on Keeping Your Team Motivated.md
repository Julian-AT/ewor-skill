## About Oliver

[Oliver Mickler](https://www.linkedin.com/in/olivermickler/) is a seasoned entrepreneur and angel investor with a strong track record of building and leading successful ventures. He is currently Senior Vice President at Unzer and co-founder and Managing Director of Tillhub GmbH.

Previously, Oliver founded and led myDriver.com, served as an Entrepreneur at Wimdu GmbH, and gained early experience in equity markets at Credit Suisse and Deutsche Bank, as well as procurement at E.ON. He studied business administration at WHU – Otto Beisheim School of Management and the University of Florida’s Warrington College of Business. Today, Oliver combines his leadership skills and financial expertise to drive innovation and growth in the technology and business sectors.
