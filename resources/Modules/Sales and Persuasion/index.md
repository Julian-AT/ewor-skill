# Sales and Persuasion

Sell your product, get talent to join your team and convince investors using research-backed sales & persuasion techniques.

This module covers the core principles of persuasion and sales that underpin the frameworks used by top industry leaders and researchers. Why it matters is simple: as a founder, you need to convince customers to buy your solution, people to join your team, and investors to back your vision—just to name a few examples. Persuasion is at the heart of every successful entrepreneur’s toolkit. Whether you're technical or non-technical, this course is designed to give you the resources you need to master and apply this essential skill.

**Duration:** 4-6 weeks
