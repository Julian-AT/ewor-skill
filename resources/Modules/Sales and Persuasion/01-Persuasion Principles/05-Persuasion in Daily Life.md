# Persuasion in Daily Life

It’s understandable if you’ve still got some reservations about improving your persuasive skill set. A useful re-frame is to see all human communication as a form of persuasion, whether it’s asking for a sale, asking someone on a date, or anything in between.  

Here’s a fun activity you can try if you’ve been feeling apprehensive about persuasion still. The next 5 times you go get a coffee/tea, ask the cashier for a 10% discount and see what happens. This is a fun way to get used to persuasion since the most common barrier to using persuasive tools is a fear of being judged or rejected. Anyone can be persuasive – now it’s your turn!
