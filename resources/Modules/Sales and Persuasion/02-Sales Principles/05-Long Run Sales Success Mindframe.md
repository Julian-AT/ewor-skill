# Long Run Sales Success Mindframe

Success in sales is mainly an inner game. Your ability to consistently show up with an enthusiastic frame of being (regardless of the results you’re achieving) is a major key to long-run success.

Here are some of my favorite nuggets of wisdom from industry experts that you can refer back to on your journey.

1. “Stay non-attached to the outcome – only 3% of people are ever ready to buy and there’s a whole lot of people who might need your help. Never get hung up on one.” – Jordan Belfort
2. “Keep your pipeline full – you will never place too much importance on a single outcome when you have multiple opportunities in a day.” – John Barrows
3. “Be like the doctor – focus on a correct diagnosis rather than pushing a prospect to buy when it’s really not in the best interest of either party. Remember, bad fit equals long-run churn.” – Josh Braun
4. “Remove all stigma and beliefs around sales being bad. Reframe sales as being a professional problem solver, knowing that your solution will really help someone escape from pain.” – Jordan Belfort
5. “Nothing is ever personal – objections are simply a reflection of your prospects’ inner state and virtually NEVER about you as an individual.” – Jordan Belfort
