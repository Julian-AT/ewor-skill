# The Purpose & Importance of Sales

Sales is often misunderstood and hated, yet it’s crucial to entrepreneurial success. While many people think of sleazy used car salesmen, sales is simply the process of diagnosing a prospect’s pain, determining if they’re the right fit for your solution, and persuading them your solution is in their best interest.

Sales is often tainted by the popular opinion that sales is all about manipulating people into buying things that aren’t in their best interests. In truth, it’s not about using sleazy tactics, hoodwinking people, or using any other form of coercion; it’s about being a solution provider that’s solving a prospect’s problem.

A successful sales process is one that communicates the desired future to a prospect who happily pays to enjoy it. A crucial note here before we roll on: You’re never selling a product or service; you’re communicating positive feelings about the future your prospect will enjoy with your solution.

Why should you care about sales? Your project requires cash and your investors want to see profitable returns on the capital they’ve put into your venture. Having a dialled-in sales and marketing machine is the ONLY way to hit those revenue targets, maintain investor confidence, and keep the wheels of your project turning. It’s the lifeblood of your venture!
