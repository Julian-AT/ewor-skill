# Daily & Weekly Reflection Checklist

The EWOR Daily and Weekly Reflection Checklists are designed to help entrepreneurs enhance productivity, maintain focus, and optimise learning through structured reflection and organisation.

The Daily Checklist includes tasks like reviewing priorities, organising desktops, managing emails and messages, and preparing for upcoming calls or deadlines. It also emphasises gratitude, daily goal tracking, and reflecting on personal habits. The Weekly Checklist focuses on a broader scope, such as evaluating top priorities, reviewing ongoing projects and OKRs (Objectives and Key Results), and preparing for the upcoming week.
