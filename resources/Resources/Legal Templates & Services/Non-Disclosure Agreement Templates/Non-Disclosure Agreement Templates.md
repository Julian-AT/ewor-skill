# Non-Disclosure Agreement Templates

A Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) is a pivotal legal contract between two parties, usually individuals or companies. In this agreement, they consent to sharing confidential information for specific purposes while committing to maintaining its confidentiality, thus playing a vital role in safeguarding sensitive data and fostering trust in diverse business interactions.


Below, you can find ready-to-use NDA templates in English and German. To use these templates, create a copy, remove the first page and fill in all the orange placeholders.
