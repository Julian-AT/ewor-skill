# Pre-Seed Investor Deck Guideline

## A Note From EWOR

At EWOR, our experience with over 1,000 pre-seed investor decks as angel investors has revealed common challenges faced by founders. These issues often result in lower valuations and diminished investor interest.

To combat this, we’re dedicated to assisting our fellows in refining their decks by sharing our insights and guidelines in our comprehensive Pre-Seed Investor Deck Guideline. 

This resource is designed to help founders navigate the complexities of investor presentations, ensuring they present their ideas in the most effective way possible. Our goal is to empower entrepreneurs with the knowledge and tools they need to succeed in the competitive world of start-up funding.

## Crafting Your Investor Deck

An investor deck is a detailed pitch deck shared with investors before meetings. It’s your first step in securing interest and meetings, but remember, it’s just the beginning of your investment journey.

Our guide helps you create a compelling pre-seed investor deck, addressing key investor questions and increasing your chances of successful follow-up meetings. By using our guide, you are gaining a myriad of insights from:

- Our experiences as angel investors, venture capitalists, and founders.
- Interviews with venture capitalists and entrepreneurs.
- Influencers like David Cowan, Brad Feld, and others.
- Established templates like Sequoia Capital’s and Y Combinator’s pitch deck templates.

Start your journey to secure funding with confidence with our guide and examples below.
